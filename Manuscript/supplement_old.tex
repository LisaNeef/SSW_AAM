\section{3 Case Studies in $\Delta$ LOD Reduction}

Figure \ref{fig:case_studies} shows the tropical wind anomalies, and associated excitation functions, for three SSW events, defined by the central dates 23 Jan 1987, 11 Feb 2001, and 24 Jan 2009.  The 1987 event was chosen because it does not exhibit the pattern of $\Delta$LOD decline before the central date that we see in the composite over all events, whereas 
Both the 2001 and 2009 events  show a strong $\Delta$LOD decline over the 2 months leading up to the central date, whereas the 1987 event does not.
